Travel

For Local Employees

Local employees (those that reside in the state of UT) will not need to book corporate travel for Product Summit. If you would like to attend in-person activities, please use your preferred method of transportation (car, bike, TRAX, FrontRunner, foot, or carpooling). Please note, you will not be reimbursed for any out-of-pocket travel expenses, mileage, or parking, or receive any type of "per diem" for your attending Product Summit in-person. Lucid may be able to assist with any travel concerns you may have. Please reach out to Sam Avila (savila@lucidchart.com) with any questions/concerns.
Lucid is located south of Salt Lake City, UT
